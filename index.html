Course Description:

This course will introduce you to creativity and criticality in a digital environment.
We are going to cover a lot of ground to show the wide range of possibilities in contemporary software and programming.
After this class you will have a better understanding of the direction(s) you would like to go in as you begin your studies
in new media as well as have a familiarity with the practices of your colleagues in the field.
